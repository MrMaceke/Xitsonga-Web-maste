Wedding Programme

Main Ceremony

10:00 AM 12:00 PM
Saturday 19 December, 2020

The main ceremony is set to kick start with the Holy Matrimony service; this is where we will declare our love and commitment to each other before God; in the presence of our loved ones.

Afterwards, a session of canape's and pre-drinks will be shared by all the guests.

Reception and Wedding Party

13:00 PM 16:00 PM
Saturday 19 December, 2020

The reception will commence and afford everyone an opportunity to reflect on the step taken and share in some speeches, well wishes and guidance from loved ones.

This will ultimately be followed by a time of jubilation and festivity with all in attendance - leading to late in the night.
